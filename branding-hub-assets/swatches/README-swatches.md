# Swatches — how to install

### Adobe (Photoshop · Illustrator · InDesign) — `BlueTec.ase`
- **Photoshop:** Window ▸ Swatches ▸ panel menu ▸ *Import Swatches…* ▸ pick `BlueTec.ase`.
- **Illustrator:** Swatches panel ▸ menu ▸ *Open Swatch Library ▸ Other Library…* ▸ `BlueTec.ase`.
- **InDesign:** Swatches panel ▸ menu ▸ *Load Swatches…* ▸ `BlueTec.ase`.

### GIMP · Inkscape · Krita — `BlueTec.gpl`
- **GIMP:** copy `BlueTec.gpl` into your `palettes` folder (Edit ▸ Preferences ▸ Folders ▸ Palettes), then refresh.
- **Inkscape:** copy into the Inkscape `palettes` folder; it appears in the palette dropdown.

### PowerPoint · Word · Outlook — `BlueTec-Office-Theme.thmx` (one-click)
Office shares one theme across all three apps. The theme sets the brand colours **and** BlueTec Geometric as the theme font.
- **PowerPoint:** Design ▸ Themes ▸ *Browse for Themes…* ▸ `BlueTec-Office-Theme.thmx`. (Or Design ▸ Variants ▸ Colors to apply just the palette.)
- **Word:** Design ▸ Themes ▸ *Browse for Themes…* ▸ `BlueTec-Office-Theme.thmx`.
- **Outlook:** uses the active Office theme — once applied in Office it is available when composing.

The brand colours then appear at the top of every colour picker under **Theme Colors**.

*Prefer to add colours by hand?* PowerPoint/Word ▸ Design ▸ Colors ▸ *Customize Colors…* → set Accent 1 = `#0077C8`, Text 2 = `#0A1628`, then Save. Full hex list below.
